Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish made with spices, marinated chicken, and fragrant basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani spices
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Cook rice until partially done.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Cook on low heat until done.
